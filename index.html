Spatio temporal cellular connectivity

Mobile ISPs (AT&T, Verizon etc.) offer coverage maps for different cellular technologies they provide (e.g., https://www.att.com/maps/wireless-coverage.html ). Also applications such as OpenSignal (http://opensignal.com/ ) make use of crowd-sourced information to derive such maps for different vendor+technology. In this project we want to study, to a certain extent, the consistency of such coverage across space and time. For example, with 4G (LTE) service does the phone always connect to 4G? Or, sometimes to 3G or 2G even when 4G coverage exists according to coverage maps? We also want to discover patterns -- getting 3G instead of 4G only at certain times and/or certain locations and/or certain movement patterns.
